Tour / Activity Description

Featured Wineries

Wineries you might visit on this tour include:

  • Hickory Creek Winery
  • Lemon Creek Winery
  • Round Barn Winery
  • Free Run Cellars
  • Butler Tasting Room
  • Founders Baroda

Inclusions

  • Chicago hotel pick-up and drop-off
  • Transportation in an air-conditioned van with aisle seating and lots of leg room
  • Visits to 3 wineries (your guide will select the wineries on the day of the tour, based on availability)
  • Wine tasting fees
  • Picnic lunch

Exclusions

  • Additional food & beverages
  • Optional gratuities for guides and winery staff
  • Optional purchases during tour

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be 21 even if I am not planning to drink? -- Yes. All guests must be 21 and have a valid photo ID (US or International drivers' licenses, passports, or other government-issued ID) in order to participate in the tour. If you do not plan on drinking, please keep in mind the ticket price will remain the same.

What happens if it rains? -- The tour will run rain or shine. In the event that the tour is cancelled due to unsafe or severe weather, you will be allowed to reschedule subject to availability or receive credit for a future tour.

What's for lunch? -- You'll enjoy a deli style gourmet lunch including sandwiches, snacks, sides and drinks.

Can you accommodate allergies or dietary restrictions? -- Given enough advance notice, your guides can usually accommodate allergies and dietary restrictions. Please be sure to note any restrictions on the Order Form.

Can we purchase additional drinks or food? -- You are welcome to purchase any additional food or drink beyond that which is included, at your own expense.

Can we purchase souvenirs during the tour? -- Guests will have the opportunity to purchase wine and other products at the wineries during the tour.

Can I leave my belongings in the van when we get off? -- Yes, but at your own risk. The van is locked anytime you leave the vehicle. However, please exercise care when deciding what to leave on board. Guides are not responsible for any items that are left in the van.

Is the van air conditioned? -- Yes, the van has air conditioning and therefore is perfect for a wine tour no matter what the temperature is outside.

Is the van wheelchair accessible? -- No, unfortunately the van is not wheelchair accessible. If someone in your group has limited mobility and you have specific questions or concerns, please call us toll-free at 888-881-3284 for more information before purchasing tickets.
