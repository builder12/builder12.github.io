Dental Implants

Dental implants are the most effective, attractive way to replace missing teeth. Implants can last a lifetime and improve your quality of life by restoring your ability to eat, speak and smile with confidence, while also stimulating bone growth and preventing facial sagging. Dr. Anthony LaVacca is a board-certified Naperville prosthodontist and a renowned implant dentist with expertise in both titanium and ceramic dental implants, as well as procedures like All-on-4® and Teeth-in-a-Day®. He relies on advanced imaging technology, techniques and materials to safely give his patients the ideal combination of function and esthetics.
